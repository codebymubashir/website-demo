Our Story

Why we built TROVE

Home / About
Since 2023

Three worlds, one standard.

TROVE started as a shared spreadsheet between two friends who kept sending each other things they'd actually buy — a jacket here, a wooden toy there, a watch strap that didn't feel disposable.

We turned that list into a store, and kept the same rule we started with: if we wouldn't keep it ourselves, it doesn't make the cut. That's the whole filter. No trend chasing, no filler SKUs.

Today TROVE covers three categories — fashion, toys, and accessories — but every piece answers to the same question before it's listed.

How A Piece Gets In

Our three-step curation process

Every product moves through the same pipeline before it reaches the shop page.

01 — Discover

We go looking

Our team sources from small workshops and established makers alike, prioritising materials and construction over brand names.

02 — Curate

We test it ourselves

Every sample is used, worn, or played with by someone on the team for at least two weeks before it's approved for listing.

03 — Deliver

We ship it right

Packaging is minimal but protective, and every order is checked once more before it leaves our warehouse.
